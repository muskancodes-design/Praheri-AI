# Business Model

## Positioning

Praheri.AI is designed to be licensed as infrastructure, not sold as a standalone consumer app. Brokers, AMCs, and banks integrate it via API as an investor engagement and protection layer on top of their existing trading, KYC, and portfolio systems. This positioning is deliberate: regulated financial institutions are far more likely to adopt a backend capability they can integrate into an experience they already control than to ask their customers to trust and install a separate third-party app.

## Revenue Model

A B2B2C SaaS model, priced per active investor under management, is the intended structure. This aligns Praheri.AI's revenue directly with the scale of protection it provides, and is a pricing pattern regulated institutions are already familiar with from other compliance and risk-management vendors — lowering the friction of a sales conversation compared to a novel pricing structure.

## Value Levers

**SIP attrition recovery.** A meaningful share of SIP cancellations stem from avoidable cash-flow timing issues rather than deliberate discontinuation. By intervening before a mandate lapses — proposing a pause rather than allowing a third failure to trigger cancellation — Praheri.AI directly protects AMC SIP revenue that would otherwise be lost to a preventable administrative failure.

**Compliance cost reduction.** Compliance teams today triage a largely undifferentiated stream of alerts. Because every case reaching a compliance officer's queue in Praheri.AI has already passed Skeptic Agent review and Guardian Agent authorization, the volume of low-value noise is reduced, and officer time is concentrated on genuinely actionable cases.

**Retention during adverse events.** Investors who experience proactive protection during a bad trade or a market downturn are less likely to abandon a platform in frustration afterward. A moment that might otherwise become a churn event becomes evidence that the platform is actively looking out for them.

**Platform differentiation.** In a brokerage market where most platforms compete primarily on pricing and interface design, the ability to credibly claim active, demonstrable investor protection is a differentiator that is difficult for a competitor to replicate quickly, since it requires architecture, not just messaging.

## Regulatory Positioning

Beyond direct commercial value to institutional partners, Praheri.AI is structured to be a credible artifact for engaging with SEBI directly: a working, auditable example of agentic AI in capital markets that separates reasoning from authorization and produces a full, regulator-exportable decision trail. As oversight expectations for AI in financial services continue to develop, this positions an adopting institution favorably relative to one relying on an opaque or unaudited AI system.

## Important Note on Projections

Any specific figures referenced in the main proposal (for example, projected SIP-lapse reduction or compliance-alert-volume reduction) are explicitly labeled as projected estimates based on the system's design logic, not measured outcomes. No production pilot has yet been conducted. Validating these figures against real data is a defined goal of the Phase 2 pilot, not a claim made about the current prototype.
